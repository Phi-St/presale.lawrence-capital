About Lawrence

The Lawrence Project will revolutionize the fundamental usage of cryptocurrencies by inventing an ecosystem of for-profit entities that all contribute to the use, fluctuation and growth of the Lawrence Currency. We will thereby generate more money for investors and the Lawrence Company to further expand its ecosystem and infrastructure.

Lawrence Coin Staking

In exchange for charging no transaction fees, the Lawrence Company will personally oversee all staking and transaction validation via dozens of servers stationed in various locations. This will ensure that the Lawrence blockchain runs regardless of local blackouts. The company will thereby net 0,25% of the total currency supply annually.

How to Buy

Create Wallet

 

Using the Widget at the top of this site, register an account and/or let the net generate wallet information for you, since the LAC blockchain will utilize unique wallet keys.

Pay

 

By connecting your own wallet from other blockchains (either USDT, BIT, ETH or SOL), you can swap 0.5USDT or an equivalent amount of other currency for one Lawrence Coin.

Claim Coins

After the main net has been launched, each user can claim the amount of Lawrence Coins they have paid for. If you have not registered an account, this will not be done automatically. Claim your Coins on this website.

Distribution

Distribution

The Lawrence Company claims 20% of the Coin.
Staking will use 20% of the Coin.
The Presale will sell up to 10% of the Coin over the course of 6 weeks.
Listings will require 10% of the Coin.
The Liquidity Pool will hold 40% of the total initial Coin quantity. Neither the Lawrence Company nor anyone else will take any money out of the pool.

There will be an initial pool of 880 million Lawrence Coins. As the price rises, the value of the coin will increase. Each year, 0.25% of the total pool will be allocated to the Lawrence Company to increase the market. This shouldn’t be a concern, however, as these generated Coins will quickly enter the circulating market, allowing more and more customers to enter the Lawrence economy.
